History of TechCon

TechCon started in 2010 as a small gathering of technology enthusiasts in Silicon Valley. Over the years, it has grown into one of the most anticipated tech conferences worldwide.

Mission

The mission of TechCon is to inspire and educate the tech community through sharing cutting-edge innovations, fostering collaboration, and promoting diversity and inclusion in technology.
